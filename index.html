Biography

I am currently an Associate Professor at the College of Computer Science & Software Engineering, Shenzhen University (SZU). I received my Ph.D. in Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing from Wuhan University in 2013. After that I did a postdoc research working with Prof. Hui Huang, who helped me start my research career in computer graphics. Before joining SZU, I worked as a software engineer at DJI Innovation and as an assistant professor at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST). My research interest generally lies in computer graphics, including texture synthesis, shape analysis, and 3D modeling. Besides graphics, I'm also interested in computer vision and deep learning techniques, especially deep generative models. I have several publication records in the top conferences and journals of both graphics and vision, including SIGGRAPH, SIGGRAPH Asia, Eurographics, CVPR, and NeurIPS.
